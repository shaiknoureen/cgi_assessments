ABOUT US

Established in 1957, our library serves as a hub for academic research, personal growth, and community engagement. We provide access to a wide range of physical and digital resources including books, journals, newspapers, e-books, and databases. We are committed to fostering a love for reading and learning, offering a variety of programs, workshops, and events for all ages. Our dedicated staff is here to assist you in finding the information you need, whether for academic purposes
