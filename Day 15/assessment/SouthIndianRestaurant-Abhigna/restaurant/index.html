Welcome to Thindi Mane — an authentic South Indian restaurant located in the heart of Bangalore. We bring the rich and diverse flavors of Karnataka together.

  • Traditional recipes prepared with locally sourced ingredients.
  • Signature dishes like Masala Dosa, Bisi Bele Bath, and Ragi Mudde.
  • Authentic South Indian meals served in a warm and welcoming ambiance.

Whether you’re craving a hearty Karnataka meal, light tiffins, or a full-course South Indian thali, we have it all. Experience the true essence of Southern hospitality with our homely flavors and impeccable service.

Why Choose Thindi Mane

At Thindi Mane, we bring you the authentic taste of South India, right in the heart of Bangalore. From age-old recipes passed down generations to freshly sourced local ingredients, every dish is crafted to perfection. Experience the warmth of home-style meals with a touch of modern dining elegance.
